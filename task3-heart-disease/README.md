# Task 3: Heart Disease Prediction

## Task Objective
The objective is to develop a binary classification model to predict whether a patient is at risk of heart disease based on their medical attributes and health data.

## Dataset Used
* **Name:** Heart Disease UCI Dataset
* **Source:** Kaggle

## Models and Techniques Applied
* **Data Preprocessing:** Handling missing values and performing EDA.
* **Models:** Logistic Regression and Decision Tree Classifier.
* **Evaluation Metrics:** Accuracy, ROC-AUC curve, and Confusion Matrix.

## Key Results and Findings
The classification model successfully predicted heart disease risk with a quantifiable accuracy score. Feature importance analysis revealed which specific medical attributes (such as chest pain type or maximum heart rate) had the most significant impact on the model's predictive outcome.
